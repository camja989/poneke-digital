What we do

Built for the NZ public sector

We specialise in open-source technology that gives government agencies control over their data, their systems, and their future.

🏛️
Government Portals

Django-powered web applications and portals built to NZISM standards, with full Microsoft 365 and Entra ID integration.

📚
Koha LMS Implementation

NZ-based implementation, migration, and support for Koha — the open-source library management system born in Aotearoa.

📊
Data Dashboards

Actionable analytics and reporting platforms built on open standards, integrated with your existing systems and data warehouse.

🤖
AI & Automation Tools

Practical AI tools for government — document redaction, workflow automation, and intelligent data processing. No hype, just utility.

System Integration

Compliance & Security

Systems designed to NZISM, PSR, and Privacy Act 2020 standards from day one — not retrofitted as an afterthought.

Why Pōneke Digital

The NZ government deserves NZ technology partners

Most government technology decisions are made by offshore vendors who understand neither te Tiriti obligations nor the realities of NZ procurement. We do.

  • 01
    Wellington timezone. Wellington relationships.

    We're on Te Whanganui-a-Tara. We attend the same meetings, understand the same constraints, and are reachable when things go wrong — not 12 hours later.

  • 02
    Open source reduces your long-term risk.

    No vendor lock-in. No licence fees that balloon as your organisation grows. Your data is yours, your systems are auditable, and you can always change partners.

  • 03
    Te reo Māori and te Tiriti by design.

    Macron support, bilingual interfaces, and hauora Māori frameworks built in from the start — not added as a checkbox before go-live.

  • 04
    Economic benefit to New Zealand.

    Every dollar spent with Pōneke Digital stays in the NZ economy. We score well on government procurement economic benefit criteria because we genuinely deliver it.
